The transform is computed with periodic boundary handling and orthonormal filters, so with a zero threshold the reconstruction is exact to machine precision. Haar is piecewise-constant (sharp on edges); Daubechies-4 is smoother (better on gradual signals). Energy compaction — most of the signal living in a few coefficients — is the property compression and denoising both exploit.
Tip: set a signal with noise, raise Levels to 4–5, then raise the threshold and watch the reconstruction stay clean while the noise drops away.
